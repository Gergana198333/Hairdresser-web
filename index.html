About Luxe Salon

Your Trusted Beauty Destination Since 2015

Welcome to Luxe Salon, where beauty meets expertise. For over 8 years, we've been transforming lives through exceptional hair and beauty services. Our passionate team is dedicated to making every client feel confident, beautiful, and valued.

Our Story

Founded by award-winning stylist Maria Gonzalez, Luxe Salon started as a small boutique salon with a big vision: to provide world-class hair services with a personal touch. What began with just 3 stylists has grown into a thriving salon with a team of certified professionals, each bringing their unique expertise and passion to every appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekend appointments and bridal services. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability. During peak seasons, please book earlier.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment may be charged 50% of the service fee. No-shows will be charged the full amount.

Do you offer a consultation before services?

Yes! We highly recommend a free consultation, especially for color services, major cuts, or treatments. You can book a consultation online or call us. This helps us understand your goals and ensure the best results.

Are your products tested on animals?

No, all our products are cruelty-free and ethically sourced. We only use premium, professional-grade products that are safe and effective.

How often should I get my hair colored?

This depends on your hair type and color choice. Generally, we recommend touching up roots every 4-6 weeks for full color and 6-8 weeks for highlights. Our stylists will provide personalized recommendations during your appointment.

How long do hair extensions last?

Hair extensions typically last 4-8 weeks depending on the method and your care routine. We provide detailed aftercare instructions. Regular maintenance appointments help extend their lifespan.

Do you offer discounts for first-time clients?

Yes! First-time clients receive a 10% discount on all services. New customers also get priority booking and complimentary consultations.

What is your loyalty rewards program?

Our loyalty program gives you 1 point for every AED spent. Earn 100 points for a free service or 50 points for AED 50 off. Members also get exclusive offers and early access to new services.

Do you have parking available?

Yes! We have ample free parking available for all clients. There are both ground-level and covered parking options. Our salon is easily accessible from main roads.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Emirates NBD), cash, and digital payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). We also offer installment plans for expensive treatments.

Can I request a specific stylist?

Absolutely! You can request your preferred stylist during booking. If they're unavailable, you can choose another date or we can recommend an equally skilled stylist.

Do you offer mobile services or home visits?

We currently operate from our salon location. However, we do offer on-site services for bridal parties and group events. Contact us for custom arrangements.
